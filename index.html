About Me


For twenty years I built cabinets for a living. In 2008 I decided to do something different. Both my wife and I returned to school. I got a degree in Finance and Economics. The challenge was well worth the effort.

I have been working as a payroll tax accountant, and controller for a PEO for the last 4 years. I decided to take on this new challenge to update my skills and ability to do some of the personal objective I have.

I enjoy wood-work, music and spending time with my family. I have two kids. A daughter who is 27, lives in Salt Lake and is a techincal writer. My second child, is a son, who attends Utah State Univesity, majoring in technical writing. They get their brains from their mother, who I've been married to for 29 years.
